Ami ke? (Who am I?)

Hello, I'm Raiyan! 👋

Welcome to my little corner of sweetness! I bake from my home kitchen in CEPZ, Chittagong. Baking started as my personal escape but quickly grew into Bakemalai—a small business fueled by genuine passion. Every cake, cupcake, and dessert I prepare is baked entirely from scratch using the richest ingredients and an extra sprinkle of love. For me, it's not just about selling sweets; it's about being a part of your celebrations and bringing those sweet moments to life!

Why Bakemalai?

Freshness First

No stale shelves here! Every single order is beautifully baked fresh just before delivery to guarantee that perfect, moist bite.

Custom Magic

Your imagination is the limit. Chat with me to beautifully design a cake layout that perfectly matches your special celebration.

Local Love

Proudly supporting local vendors and using the finest authentic ingredients in and around the Chittagong area.
