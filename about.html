Ami ke?

Hello, I'm Samanta! 👋

Welcome to my little corner of sweetness! I bake from my home kitchen in Patenga, Chittagong. Baking started as my personal escape but quickly grew into Bakemalai: a small business fueled by genuine passion. Every cake every cupcake every single scrumptious dessert that emerges from my oven is baked from scratch with the richest most luxurious ingredients and of course an extra heaping spoonful of love! For me, it's not just about selling sweets; it's about being a part of your celebrations and bringing those sweet moments to life!

Why Bakemalai?

🍫 Freshness

No stale shelves here! Every single order is beautifully baked fresh just before delivery to guarantee that perfect, moist bite.

🎨 Custom Orders

Your imagination is the limit. Chat with me to beautifully design a cake layout that perfectly matches your special celebration.

🌿 Local Ingredients

Proudly supporting local vendors and using the finest authentic ingredients in and around the Chittagong area.
